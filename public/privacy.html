Privacy Policy

Click4Cuti — Leave Management Application
Effective date: 21 June 2026 · Last updated: 21 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VHA Cognitive (M) Sdn Bhd (“we”, “us”, “our”), the operator of the Click4Cuti leave-management application and website (the “Service”), collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data. We are committed to handling your personal data in accordance with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) and applicable data-protection laws.

Click4Cuti is a business-to-business (B2B) workforce application provided to employers (HR agencies and companies). If you use Click4Cuti as an employee, your employer is the party that determines how your data is processed (the data controller) and we act as its service provider (data processor). Please also refer to your employer’s own privacy notice.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data, primarily provided by your employer or by you when using the Service:

  • Account & identity data: full name, work email address, employee ID, role (employee, manager, admin), and authentication credentials.
  • Employment data: company/agency, department, position, date of joining, years of service, work schedule, and leave entitlement tier.
  • Leave & attendance data: leave applications, leave types, dates, balances, approval status, reasons, and related warning records.
  • Documents you upload: supporting files such as medical certificates or other documents you attach to a leave application.
  • Usage & device data: log data, app activity, IP address, device type and operating system, used to operate and secure the Service.

We do not collect payment-card data, precise location, or contacts from your device. The mobile app may request camera and file-access permissions solely so you can capture or attach supporting documents; these are used only when you choose to attach a file.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage accounts and authenticate users.
  • To process, route, and record leave applications and approvals.
  • To calculate leave entitlements and balances and enforce applicable employment-law rules.
  • To send notifications related to leave requests, approvals, and account activity.
  • To maintain audit logs, secure the Service, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.
  • To provide support and improve the reliability and functionality of the Service.

3. Legal basis for processing

We process personal data on the basis of: performance of the contract between us and your employer; your employer’s legitimate interests in administering leave and complying with employment law; legal obligations; and, where required, consent.

4. How we share information

We do not sell personal data. We disclose personal data only:

  • Within your organisation: to authorised managers, approvers, and administrators of your employer who need it to process leave.
  • To service providers (sub-processors): hosting and cloud-storage providers that operate our infrastructure (for example, cloud server and object-storage providers) under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.
  • For legal reasons: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as your employer maintains an active account and as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal and record-keeping obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Upon termination of an employer’s account, data is deleted or anonymised within a reasonable period unless retention is required by law.

6. Security

We apply technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, tenant isolation, hashed credentials, and audit logging. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and to address vulnerabilities responsibly.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request to access, correct, or update your personal data, and in certain cases to limit or withdraw consent for processing. As leave data is administered by your employer, please direct such requests to your employer’s HR/administrator in the first instance, or contact us using the details below and we will assist or route your request appropriately.

8. Children

The Service is intended for use by employees in a workplace context and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 16.

9. International data transfers

Where personal data is processed or stored outside your country, we take steps to ensure it remains protected in accordance with this Policy and applicable law.

10. Account & data deletion

Employee accounts are created and removed by your employer’s administrator. To request deletion of your account or personal data, contact your employer’s administrator or email us at the address below; we will action verified requests in accordance with applicable law and our obligations to your employer.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, by additional notice.
